Steak & Grill

Steak and Grill is a legendary Long Island restaurant that has been serving up delicious steaks, seafood, and more for over 100 years. This family-run establishment is a beloved fixture in the community and has earned a reputation for its mouth-watering menu, warm hospitality, and charming atmosphere. From the moment you step inside, you'll feel like you're part of the family, as the staff takes great care to make sure you have an exceptional dining experience.
